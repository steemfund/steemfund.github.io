Steem Witness proposal and information


My main motivation to start running a witness was to take part in the ongoing discussion about the future of steemit at what I thought was a critical time. My intention was, and still is, to promote a better distribution of rewards without compromising the stability of the network.

If you are a new to Delegated proof of stake (DPOS) and you are wondering what a witness is I invite you to read this post, which outlines the importance of the steemit system and how we can all influence the future of the platfrom.

In this sense, my decision to become a witness is as philosophical as it is technical, I would like to represent my voters in the “witness table” and help the steemit founders and developers improve the platform for all its existing and future users. 

I also see steemit expanding into different communities and for that we need to promote other languages. I support language tags and I try to always write, read and vote for posts under the #spanish tag. To help spanish users find and promote their own posts I created @steemiobot twitter account and telegram channel, @steemiobot automatically broadcasts all the posts published with the #spanish tag.

I am also a long time supporter of the Venezuelan community on Steemit. It is my believe that Steemit can really make a difference on third world countries. I have carried out Facebook campaigns to attract more venezuelans to steemit.com, I also vote and promote the venezuelan trail of @provenezuela and I have created the Venezuelan chainbb sub-forum: chainbb.com/f/venezuela
